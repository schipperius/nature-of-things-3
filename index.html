the Nature of Things

– patterns, structures, processes and such

The Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao. [1]


[2]

The Cosmos is all that is or was or ever will be. [3]


[4]

The Tao is the understanding or intuitive knowing of 'life' or present awareness. Tao signifies the primordial essence or fundamental nature of the universe. Tao signifys 'way, 'path' or 'route'. [5]

The word science comes to us from the Latin word scientia meaning 'knowledge' and originally referred to a type of knowledge that can be communicated and shared like the knowledge about the nature of things. [6]
