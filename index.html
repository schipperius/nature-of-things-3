the Nature of Things

– patterns, structures, processes and such

The Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao.

Laoz “Tao Te Ching”, 6th century BCE [1]

[2]

The Cosmos is all that is or was or ever will be.

Carl Sagan “Cosmos”, 1980. [3]

[4]

Questions about the origin, nature and meaning of life are as old as humanity itself.

Fritjof Capra et al. “The Systems View of Life”, 2014. [5]

The Nature of Things is an exploration of the myriad ways of knowing the world. Just trying to make sense of things and what better way to start than with spirituality juxtaposed with science.
