Questions about the origin, nature and meaning of life are as old as humanity itself.

Fritjof Capra et al. “The Systems View of Life”, 2014. [5]

Like many folks, I have always wondered where I came from, why I'm here, where I'm going, and when will I get there. In trying to answer these questions, I have stumbled upon a great number of people who have before me tried to answer these same questions. In the course of learning these different interpretations, I have been frequently distracted by the seemingly conflicting and contradictory perspectives that exist.

This website, the Nature of Things, is my personal exploration of the myriad ways of knowing about the world, I'm just trying to make some sense of things.
